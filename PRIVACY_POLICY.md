# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** November 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Easy Spades handles your information when you use the bot on Discord.

## 1. Information Collection
- Easy Spades only collects data necessary for gameplay, such as usernames, scores, and game actions.
- No personal or sensitive information is intentionally collected.

## 2. Data Usage
- Collected data is used solely to provide and improve game functionality.
- Game logs may be used for debugging and analytics to enhance user experience.

## 3. Data Sharing
- Easy Spades does not share, sell, or disclose your data to third parties.
- Data may be shared only if required by law or to enforce fair play.

## 4. Data Security
- Reasonable measures are taken to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.
- However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 5. User Rights
- You may request deletion of your game data by contacting the bot administrator.
- For questions or concerns, contact the bot administrator.

## 6. Changes to Policy
- This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of Easy Spades constitutes acceptance of any changes.
